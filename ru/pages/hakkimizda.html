Our Story

Insulating
the Future

Inspired by nature, we produce sustainable, eco-friendly, and health-conscious insulation solutions. At Marterm, we protect not just buildings, but the future itself.

Corporate

We, as Marterm...

We were established with the aim of bringing a breath of fresh air to the insulation sector, placing energy efficiency and sustainability at the core of our operations. In a time when our planet faces climate crises, we are proud to offer a completely natural, recyclable, and healthy alternative to synthetic products that harm the environment.

By conducting our R&D studies in the accredited laboratories of Gaziantep University, we discovered the superior acoustic and thermal insulation capacity of our national asset, goat hair. By combining this discovery with our patented production algorithms, we’ve achieved a performance increase of up to 60% compared to traditional products in the sector.
